# Command: /pr:follow-up

## Description
Draft follow-up sequences for journalists who haven't responded to the initial pitch.

## Instructions

1. Ask for: which journalists need follow-up, how many days since initial pitch, any new information available (new data, updated spokesperson availability, additional access to offer).

2. Apply the follow-up cadence rules from `pr-outreach-execution.md`:
   - First follow-up: 2-3 business days after initial pitch
   - Second follow-up: 4-5 business days after first follow-up
   - No third follow-up

3. For each journalist needing follow-up, draft:
   - **First follow-up**: Brief, adds new value if possible, low pressure
   - **Second follow-up**: Final check-in, easy out for journalist

4. Remind user: Do not follow up journalists who have explicitly passed. Do not send follow-ups over the weekend.

5. Flag if any journalist has exceeded the two-follow-up limit — note them as closed and move on.

## Output
Follow-up email drafts, organized by journalist and follow-up number. Note any journalists who should be closed out.
